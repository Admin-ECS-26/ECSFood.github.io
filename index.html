About E C S

Emergency Culinary Solutions was founded with the goal of delivering rapid-response food service solutions during times of crisis. Since our first deployment after Hurricane Michael, we have built a legacy of providing quality meals to those who need it most.

Our team operates state-of-the-art mobile kitchens capable of preparing and serving up to 5,000 meals a day. Fully self-sustaining units allow us to perform in challenging environments, both domestically and internationally.

Our Mission
